## Polar form of Z


$$ Z = |Z| e^{i\theta} ,\qquad  Where \;e^{i\theta} = Cos\theta + iSin\theta$$

$a = |Z| Cos\theta$
$a = |Z| Sin\theta$
Z = a + bi
Z = $|Z| Cos\theta$ + $i |Z| Sin\theta$
Z = |Z| ($Cos\theta + i Sin\theta$ )
$Z = |Z| e^{i\ \theta}$
